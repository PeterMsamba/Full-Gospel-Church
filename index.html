The Fullness of Time

Galatians 4:4

"For the word of God is alive and active…"

Beloved God is never late. What felt like centuries of delay was actually divine preparation. When Jesus came, heaven moved with precision and speed. Divine acceleration begins when God’s appointed time meets human need. Trust that your season is not delayed—it is aligning.

Prayer:

Lord, help me trust Your timing. Strengthen my faith as I wait, knowing that when You move, You move perfectly. In Jesus name. Amen.
